What is Term Class?

A structured 10-week program within a 3-month term, offering clear lesson plans, objectives, and a student progress report at the term's end. Available for various activities such as Badminton, Ballet, Basketball, and more, it focuses on skill progression and development.

  • Goal-Oriented and Structured: Ideal for members who prefer a structured approach.
  • Skill Development Focused: Enhance skills systematically over a period.
  • Commitment-Ready: Fixed schedule for 10 weeks.
  • Progress Tracking: Members receive feedback and reports for improvement.
